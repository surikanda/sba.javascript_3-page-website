What is BMI ?

Body mass index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women.

BMI Comaprision
BMI < 18.5 Underweight
BMI 18.5 - 24.9 Normal weight
BMI 25 - 29.9 Overweight
BMI >= 30 Obesity
